Dear Guests,

We are happy to welcome you in our hotel.The legendary story starts with four friends and a mission.The Grand Hotel was founded in the late ‘50s, to help solve the housing shortage for students. By also offering rooms to hotel guests in the building, The Grand Hotel is – to this day – able to provide students enjoyable and affordable student housing. And that makes for a unique mixture of people that’ll never bore you.
